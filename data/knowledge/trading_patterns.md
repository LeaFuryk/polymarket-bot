# Trading Patterns

## Key Principles for 5-Min BTC Candle Markets

1. **24h BTC change is NOT predictive** for 5-min candles. ~40% of 5-min candles go opposite to the daily trend. Do not anchor on the daily direction.

2. **Micro-momentum matters**: The last 3-6 five-minute candles are the best predictor of the next candle. Look for momentum continuation or exhaustion patterns.

3. **Mean reversion after 3+ same-direction candles**: After 3 or more consecutive candles in one direction, there is an increased probability of reversal. BUT this is context-dependent - see Trend Context Rules below.

4. **Spread dynamics**: When Up/Down mid prices diverge significantly from summing to $1.00, there may be a pricing inefficiency to exploit.

5. **Volume confirmation**: Higher volume on directional candles confirms momentum; low volume moves are less reliable.

6. **Flat candles are common**: BTC can remain at the same price across multiple 5-min candles. When BTC Open = BTC Close, the "up" outcome wins by default. This creates opportunities when "up" is underpriced during sideways action.

## Strategy Selection by Market Regime

### Choppy/Range-Bound (BTC range <$50 over 30 min):
- PRIMARY: Mean reversion at full confidence (0.60-0.65)
- Look for underpriced "up" in flat markets
- Standard position sizing (50-60 shares)

### Trending (6+ consecutive candles, >$100 move):
- PRIMARY: Trend-following at good prices (0.22-0.55)
- AVOID: Mean reversion (high failure rate in these conditions)
- Profit-taking: exit at 0.66-0.93 when bought at 0.22-0.51

### Exhaustion (>$150 move in 2-3 candles):
- AVOID: Chasing continuation at >0.60
- PREFERRED: HOLD and wait for consolidation
- Counter-trend only if entry <0.40 and strong exhaustion signals

## Entry Pricing Discipline (STRICT)

*Mean Reversion (fade trades)*:
- Excellent: <0.30 (size up to 70 shares)
- Good: 0.30-0.45 (standard 50-60 shares)
- Marginal: 0.45-0.60 (reduce to 40-50 shares)
- Avoid: >0.60 (insufficient edge)

*Trend-Following (continuation trades)*:
- Excellent: 0.22-0.45 (size up to 60-65 shares)
- Good: 0.45-0.55 (standard 50-60 shares)
- Acceptable: 0.55-0.65 (reduce to 45-50 shares)
- NEVER: >0.65 (prevents catastrophic losses)

## Exit Discipline

- When position moves 30-50% in your favor, consider profit-taking
- Exit targets: 0.66-0.93 range when bought at 0.22-0.51
- With <120s remaining and profitable position, strongly consider exit
- In strong trends (6+ consecutive), can hold longer for larger gains
- Early loss-cutting: If setup clearly wrong and >120s remaining, exit at small loss

## Pre-Trade Checklist (MANDATORY)

1. **30-Minute Trend Check**: Calculate BTC move over last 6-8 candles
2. **Momentum Direction Check**: Count last 6 candles to verify direction
3. **Strategy Selection**: Match strategy to regime (choppy vs trending vs exhaustion)
4. **Entry Price Validation**: Must meet pricing thresholds or automatic HOLD
5. **Time Management**: No new entries with <90s remaining

## Key Failure Modes to Avoid

1. Using mean reversion in trending markets
2. Buying at >0.65 when edge requires <0.55
3. Repeating the same failed setup without adaptation
4. Not checking 30-minute context before trades
5. Chasing continuation after large move (>$150) at expensive prices
6. Trading with <90s remaining
