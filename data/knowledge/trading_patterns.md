# Trading Patterns

## Key Principles for 5-Min BTC Candle Markets

1. **24h BTC change is NOT predictive** for 5-min candles. ~40% of 5-min candles go opposite to the daily trend. Do not anchor on the daily direction.

2. **Micro-momentum matters**: The last 3-6 five-minute candles are the best predictor of the next candle. Look for momentum continuation or exhaustion patterns.

3. **Mean reversion after 3+ same-direction candles**: After 3 or more consecutive candles in one direction, there is an increased probability of reversal. BUT this is context-dependent - see Trend Context Rules below.

4. **Spread dynamics**: When Up/Down mid prices diverge significantly from summing to $1.00, there may be a pricing inefficiency to exploit.

5. **Volume confirmation**: Higher volume on directional candles confirms momentum; low volume moves are less reliable.

6. **Flat candles are common**: BTC can remain at the same price across multiple 5-min candles. When BTC Open = BTC Close, the "up" outcome wins by default. This creates opportunities when "up" is underpriced during sideways action.

### When Mean Reversion Works (75%+ win rate):
- BTC is range-bound or choppy over 30+ minutes
- Recent moves are small ($4-$40 range)
- No clear 30-minute directional bias
- Entry prices <0.45 (best edge)

### When Mean Reversion Fails (25% win rate):
- BTC in sustained trend (>$100 move in 20-30 minutes)
- Consecutive candles getting larger (momentum acceleration)
- Multiple failed reversal attempts already occurred
- Strong directional pressure overrides short-term exhaustion

## Profit-Taking and Exit Discipline

**Session 7 Key Learning**: Smart exits are as important as smart entries.

### When to Exit Profitably:
- Position moves 15-20% in your favor (e.g., bought at 0.41, sell at 0.78)
- Time remaining <120s and position is profitable
- Market shows signs of reversal but you're still in profit
- Better to lock in +$20-30 than risk holding to resolution

### When to Cut Losses:
- Position moves significantly against you AND setup looks wrong
- Time remaining >120s but market regime has clearly changed
- Example: Bought DOWN at 0.23 expecting continuation, but BTC shows UP momentum
- Taking -$12 loss early can prevent -$22 loss at resolution

## UPDATED Trading Rules

### Pre-Trade Checklist (MANDATORY):

**1. 30-Minute Trend Check (MANDATORY)**:
- Calculate BTC move over last 6-8 candles
- If >$100 in one direction: STRONG TREND
  - Mean reversion: reduce confidence by 0.20 or HOLD
  - Trend-following: PREFERRED strategy if entry <0.55
- If >$150 in one direction: VERY STRONG TREND
  - Mean reversion: HOLD (do not trade)
  - Trend-following: Primary strategy, enter at <0.50
- If <$50 total range: CHOPPY
  - Mean reversion: Standard confidence (0.60-0.65)
  - Trend-following: Avoid (no sustained momentum)

**2. Recent Move Check (CRITICAL)**:
- If BTC just moved >$150 in last 2-3 candles: EXHAUSTION RISK
  - Don't chase continuation at >0.60
  - Wait for pullback or consolidation
  - Consider counter-trend if entry <0.40
- If moves are consistent $50-100 range: HEALTHY TREND
  - Trend-following at good prices (<0.55) is optimal

**3. Consecutive Candle Count**:
- 3-4 consecutive: Ambiguous (could reverse or continue)
- 5-6 consecutive: Strong signal
  - In choppy market: Mean reversion setup
  - In trending market: Trend-following setup
- 7+ consecutive: Extreme (use caution, reduce size)
- **After large move**: Even if only 2-3 consecutive, if total move >$150, treat as exhaustion

**4. Entry Pricing Discipline (STRICT)**:

*Mean Reversion (fade trades)*:

- Excellent: 0.23-0.45 (size up to 60-65 shares)

- Acceptable: 0.55-0.65 (reduce to 45-50 shares)
- Avoid: >0.65 (wait for pullback or HOLD)
- **CRITICAL**: After large move (>$150), avoid >0.60 entirely

**5. Exit Discipline (UPDATED)**:
- When position moves 15-20% in your favor, consider profit-taking
- Exit targets: 0.78-0.88 range when bought at 0.23-0.51
- With <120s remaining and profitable position, strongly consider exit
- Don't hold through obvious adverse setups hoping for miracle
- Early loss-cutting: If setup clearly wrong and >120s remaining, exit at small loss
- **NEW**: Profit-taking exits have 100% success rate in Session 7 (3 wins)

**6. Recent Pattern Performance**:
- If last 2 similar setups failed: STOP taking that pattern
- If 0-2 or worse in session: Reduce all sizes by 30%

## Pattern Recognition Updates

### Sustained Trend Signature (REFINED):
- 6+ consecutive same-direction candles
- BTC moving >$100 over 30 minutes
- Candles maintaining size or growing
- Failed reversal attempts get rejected quickly

**Trading Response**:
- PRIMARY: Trend-following at good prices (0.23-0.55)
- AVOID: Mean reversion (78% failure rate in these conditions)
- Position sizing: Standard (50-60 shares)
- After large single move (>$150), wait for consolidation
- **NEW**: Take profits at 0.78-0.88 when bought at 0.23-0.51

### Momentum Exhaustion Pattern (CRITICAL):
- BTC moves >$150 in 2-3 candles
- Token prices reflect continuation (>0.60)
- High risk of reversal or consolidation

**Trading Response**:
- AVOID: Chasing continuation at >0.60
- CONSIDER: Counter-trend if entry <0.40 and strong exhaustion signals
- PREFERRED: HOLD and wait for next setup
- If must trade: Reduce size by 30-50%

### Choppy/Range-Bound Signature:
- Mixed UP/DOWN candles
- BTC range <$50 over 30 minutes
- No clear directional momentum
- Small candle bodies

**Trading Response**:
- PRIMARY: Mean reversion at full confidence (0.60-0.65)
- Look for underpriced "up" in flat markets
- Standard position sizing
- Can hold through small adverse moves
