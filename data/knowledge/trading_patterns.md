# Trading Patterns

## Key Principles for 5-Min BTC Candle Markets

1. **24h BTC change is NOT predictive** for 5-min candles. ~40% of 5-min candles go opposite to the daily trend. Do not anchor on the daily direction.

2. **Micro-momentum matters**: The last 3-6 five-minute candles are the best predictor of the next candle. Look for momentum continuation or exhaustion patterns.

3. **Mean reversion after 3+ same-direction candles**: After 3 or more consecutive candles in one direction, there is an increased probability of reversal.

4. **Spread dynamics**: When Up/Down mid prices diverge significantly from summing to $1.00, there may be a pricing inefficiency to exploit.

5. **Volume confirmation**: Higher volume on directional candles confirms momentum; low volume moves are less reliable.

## Patterns to Watch For
- Consecutive same-direction candles → potential reversal
- MA5/MA12 crossover on BTC candle closes → trend shift
- Large body candles followed by small body → momentum exhaustion
- Price near MA50 → potential support/resistance
