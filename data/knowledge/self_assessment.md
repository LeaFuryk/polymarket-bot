## Known Biases to Avoid

1. **24h Anchoring Bias**: Do NOT let the daily BTC change dominate reasoning. Low predictive value for 5-min outcomes.

2. **Bearish Bias on Red Days**: Avoid betting bearish on every candle just because the daily candle is red.

3. **Low Confidence Trading**: Trades at 0.52-0.58 confidence are low quality. Only trade when confidence >= 0.6.

4. **Overtrading**: Not every cycle needs a trade. A well-timed HOLD is better than a low-edge trade.

5. **Ignoring Flat Markets**: When BTC is consolidating, "up" wins by default. Don't force directional calls.

6. **Mean Reversion Overconfidence**: Mean reversion is probabilistic, not guaranteed. Entry price is CRITICAL.

7. **Ignoring Trend Context**: Trading mean reversion in isolation without checking if BTC is in a sustained trend.

8. **Pattern Rigidity**: Repeating the same failed setup. When a pattern fails twice consecutively, conditions have changed.

9. **Momentum Chasing**: Chasing continuation after large moves (>$150) at expensive prices (>0.50).

10. **Entry Discipline Violations**: NEVER enter trend-following at >0.50 or mean reversion at >0.45.

11. **Extreme Price Entry**: NEVER buy at >0.50 for trend-following regardless of setup strength. The risk/reward is catastrophically bad.

12. **Exit Hesitation**: Holding losing positions hoping for reversal. When down >25% with >180s remaining, exit immediately.

13. **Profit Protection Failure**: Letting winning positions turn into losses. When up >25% with 120-180s remaining, EXIT immediately.

14. **Choppy Market Trading**: Trading in sideways/range-bound markets with no clear momentum. HOLD is often the best decision.

15. **Small Move Overconfidence**: Moves of $10-30 are essentially coin flips. Don't trade them at high confidence.

16. **Marginal Entry Pricing**: Entering mean reversion at 0.37-0.45 is marginal edge. Need <0.30 for strong edge.

17. **Panic Selling**: Selling at extreme losses with <60s remaining locks in maximum loss. Hold or exit earlier.

18. **NEW - Exit Greed**: Waiting for extreme exit prices (0.10-0.20) often results in reversals. Take profits at 0.25-0.35.

19. **NEW - Wrong Strategy Selection**: Using trend-following (buying continuation) after 3+ consecutive candles. Should use mean reversion instead.
